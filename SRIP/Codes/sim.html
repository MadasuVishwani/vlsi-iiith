Simulator for D Flip Flop

How to use the simulator

    Components Required

  1. 4 PMOS
  2. 4 NMOS
  3. Ground
  4. Vdd
  5. Input Pins
  6. Output Pins

    Connecting the Components

  1. Make the connections with wire. The connections would be similar to the example in theory except that instead of C and D we will have A complement and B complement
  2. Connect both the pairs of PMOS in parallel i.e the source and drain of both should be connected (shown below)
  3. The two parallel combination is placed in series between Vdd and output node
  4. Connect both the pairs of NMOS in seriesi.e. the source of one getsconnected to the drain of other
  5. The two series connection is then placed in paralle between output node and ground
  6. Connect a capacitor between output node and ground

Obeservation and Simulation

  1. Press the simulate button on the top
  2. Observe the graph nd verify that it works as XOR/XNOR gate
